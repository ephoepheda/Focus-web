About Us

FOCUS AMU (Fellowship of Christian University Students) is a vibrant community at Arba Minch University.

We are dedicated to helping students grow spiritually, build strong relationships, and develop leadership skills that will serve them throughout their lives. Through worship, study, and service, we strive to make a positive impact on campus and beyond.

Whether you are looking for a place to belong, seeking answers, or want to serve others, you are welcome here.
